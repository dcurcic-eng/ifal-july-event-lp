Good to Know
Questions,answered

Everything you need to know before you arrive.

What is vibroacoustic therapy?
Low-frequency sound vibrations are delivered through a specialized sound bed so you feel the music in your body. It gently calms the nervous system and is completely non-invasive, and you stay fully clothed throughout.
Do I need any experience?
None at all. Wear comfortable clothes and arrive hydrated, and that's all you need to take part.
Are the demo sessions really free?
Yes. Complimentary turns on the BioVibe sound bed are available to guests throughout the event.
Is it safe for everyone?
It's gentle and safe for most adults. It is not recommended with a pacemaker or implant, active blood clots, or during pregnancy, so please ask our team if you're unsure.
What should I bring?
Just yourself in comfortable clothes. Light snacks and beverages are provided.
How do I reserve?
Spots are limited, so simply RSVP using the form above to reserve your place.
